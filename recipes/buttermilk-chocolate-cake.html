Buttermilk Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • Flour, 2 cups
  • Sugar, 2 cups
  • Canola oil, ½ cup
  • Cocoa, 4 tbsp + 4 tbsp
  • Water, 1 cup
  • Eggs, 2
  • Buttermilk, 1 cup + 6 tbsp
  • Baking soda, 1 tsp
  • Vanilla, 1 tsp
  • Butter, ½ cup + ½ cup
  • Powdered sugar, 1 boy

Credit Where Due

A Bridget holiday classic

Kitchen Gear

  • Pot
  • Baking pan about 11x17 inches

Commentary

  • Be sure to front the cake while it's hot out of the oven
  • This is a favorite to make at the start of a holiday to leave around the kitchen for passersby

Bake Cake

  1. To pot add
    • Canola oil
    • Cocoa
    • Butter, ½ cup
    • Vanilla
    • Water
    and bring to a boil, stir regularly
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour and sugar then add stir in boiled ingredients
  3. Stir in
    • Beaten eggs
    • Buttermilk, 1 cup
    • Baking soda
    • Vanilla
  4. Pour into a greased + floured 11x17 pan and bake at 350° F

Prepare Frosting

  1. To pot add
    • Buttermilk, 6 tbsp
    • Cocoa, 4 tbsp
    • Butter, ½ cup
    and bring to a boil
  2. Remove from heat and stir in powdered sugar

Frost and Serve

  1. Frost the cake when hot out of the oven
  2. Let cool then slice as desired
  3. Enjoy!
