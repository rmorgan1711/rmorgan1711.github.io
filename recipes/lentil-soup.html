Complete-Meal Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • shallots, 2 OR yellow (sweet) onion, 1
  • olive oil, about 4 tbsp
  • lentils, 2 cups
  • potatoes, 2 medium sized
  • cold water, 5 cups
  • garlic, 2+ cloves
  • cumin, 2 tbsp
  • carrots, 2 medium sized
  • chicken (any cut), ½ pound | finely chopped and browned
  • green onion, to taste
  • salt & pepper, to taste

Credit Where Due

From page 366 of Pamela Druckerman's wonderful book Bringing Up Bébé.
ISBN-10: 0143122967 | ISBN-13: 978-0143122968

Kitchen Gear

  • Large sauce pan with top OR pot with top
  • Vegetable peeler

Commentary

  • Prepare (peel, wash, chop) raw shallots/onion, potato, and carrots in advance and set aside

Cook

  1. Add some olive oil to pan/pot and bring to medium heat
  2. Finely dice shallots/onion and sauté in pan/pot
  3. Peel, wash, and medium chop potatoes
  4. Peel and chop carrots
  5. To pan/pot, add in order
    1. Lentils
    2. Potatoes
    3. Cold water
    4. Garlic
    5. Pepper
    6. Cumin
    7. Carrots
  6. Bring to a boil
  7. Cover and simmer over low heat until potatoes and lentils are soft (about 45 min. Add water as needed.
  8. Add salt to taste
  9. Finely chop and brown chicken in olive oil
  10. Add browned chicken to pot
  11. Ladle soup into bowls and top with chopped green onion to taste
  12. Enjoy!
