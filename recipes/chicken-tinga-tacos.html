Chicken Tinga Tacos

Ingredients

  • chicken,1 lb
  • fire roasted tomatoes, 1 can
  • chipotle peppers, 2 - 3
  • yellow (sweet) onion, 1 whole
  • red onion, 1/2
  • garlic, 2 cloves
  • chicken broth, 1 can
  • olive oil, 1 tbsp
  • cotija cheese, to taste
  • oregano, 1 tbsp
  • ground cumin, 1 tbsp
  • taco seasoning, 1/4 cup
  • butter, 2 tbsp
  • corn tortillas, 12

Credit Where Due

This recipe is inspired by (and nearly identical to) a recipe from The Minimalist Kitchen as mentioned in the pinch of yum blog.

Kitchen Gear

  • Instant Pot or pressure cooker
  • pan/skillet, 2
  • blender

Commentary

  • Start the chicken about 15 minutes before everything else
  • Cook tortillas while preparing sauce and keep warm in foil and/or in oven at 170° F
  • There are infinite wonderful toping options. Consider sprinkling any of the following as garnish
    • cilantro
    • green onion or chives
    • squeezed lime juice
    • fresh diced basil

Cook Chicken

  1. In Instant Pot or pressure cooker, add
    • 1 lb of chicken
    • chicken broth until chicken is nearly covered
    • taco seasoning, to taste (usually 20 shakes of the container or so)
  2. Mix ingredients until distributed evenly
  3. Cook 25 minutes under pressure
  4. Chop or shred when cooked

Cook Sauce

  1. Add 1 tbsp olive oil to pan and set to medium heat
  2. Add 1 roughly chopped yellow onion and stir occasionally until wilted
  3. Add 2 cloves garlic and 2 - 3 chipotle peppers, medium chopped. Stir and sizzle for 1-2 min.
  4. Add
    • Fire roasted tomatoes, 1 can
    • oregano, 1 tbsp
    • ground cumin, 1 tbsp
    • chicken broth, rest of can or 1/4 cup
    and sizzle on medium heat for 8 min
  5. Remove from heat and let cool until comfortable to touch
  6. Blend until smooth
  7. Return to pan and stir in chicken (chopped or shredded)
  8. Cook on medium heat for 5 min then reduce to low until ready to serve

Cook Tortillas

  1. Bring pan to medium-high heat
  2. Rub butter over hot pan and lay tortillas in pan
  3. When tortillas brown on bottom, rub butter on top and flip
  4. Remove from heat when brown to taste on both sides
  5. Repeat until all tortillas cooked

Combine and Serve

  • Dice red onion
  • Top tortilla with chicken & sauce mixture
  • Sprinkle red onion and cotija cheese to taste
  • Enjoy!
