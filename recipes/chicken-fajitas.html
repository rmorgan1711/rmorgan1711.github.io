Chicken Fajitas

  • chicken, 1 lb
  • sweet or bell peppers, 1.5 cups chopped
  • white onion, 1 whole
  • green onion or chives, 1 bundle (topping to taste)
  • red onion, 1/2 (topping to taste)
  • cotija cheese, topping to taste
  • taco seasoning, 1/4 cup
  • chicken broth, 1/2 cup (or water, 1/2 cup + salt, 2 tbsp)
  • vegetable oil, 3 tbsp
  • lemon or lime juice, 2 tbsp
  • corn tortillas, 9

Kitchen Gear

  • cast iron skillet
  • large pan

Commentary

  • Cook tortillas while preparing fajitas and keep warm in foil and/or in oven at 170 degrees F
  • There are infinite wonderful toping options. Consider sprinkling any of the following as garnish
    • cilantro
    • squeezed lime juice
    • fresh diced basil

Marinate Chicken

  1. Cut chicken into cubes or strips of desired size
  2. To bowl or zip lock bag add
    • cubed chicken
    • chicken broth (or water + salt)
    • taco seasoning
    • lemon or lime juice
  3. Marinate at least 1 hour refrigerated before cooking

Cook Meal

  1. Add vegetable oil to skillet and bring to medium heat
  2. Add marinated chicken to hot skillet, stir occasionally
  3. Dice peppers and white onion to desired size
  4. When chicken nearly cooked to taste, add peppers and white onion
  5. Reduce to medium-low heat and stir occasionally until vegetables cooked to taste
  6. Reduce to low heat until ready to serve

Cook Tortillas

  1. Bring pan to medium-high heat
  2. Rub butter over hot pan and lay tortillas in pan
  3. When tortillas brown on bottom, rub butter on top and flip
  4. Remove from heat when brown to taste on both sides
  5. Repeat until all tortillas cooked

Combine and Serve

  • Finely dice red and green onion
  • Top tortilla with fajita mixture
  • Sprinkle red onion, green onion, and cotija cheese to taste
  • Enjoy!
