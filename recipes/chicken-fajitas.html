Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients

  • chicken, 1 lb
  • sweet or bell peppers, 1.5 cups chopped
  • white onion, 1 whole
  • black beans, 1 can (optional)
  • corn tortillas, 9
  • fajita seasoning
  • olive oil, 2 tbsp
  • butter, 2 tbsp
  • Toppings to taste
    • green onion or chives, 1 bundle
    • red onion, 1/2
    • cotija cheese
    • lime wedges or juice

Kitchen Gear

  • cast iron skillet
  • large pan

Commentary

  • Cook tortillas while preparing fajitas and keep warm in foil and/or in oven at 170 degrees F

Marinate Chicken

  1. Cut chicken into cubes or strips of desired size
  2. To bowl or zip lock bag add
    • Cubed chicken
    • Fajita seasoning
    • Olive oil
  3. Marinate at least 1 hour refrigerated before cooking

Cook Fajitas

  1. Roughly chop white onion and set aside
  2. Slice peppers length wise and open to remove seeds and pith (veins)
  3. Slice peppers into long strips
  4. Add 1 tbsp butter to skillet and bring to medium heat
  5. Add onions to hot skillet and cook until translucent
  6. Add peppers to skillet and cook until tender and charred
  7. Remove onion and peppers from skillet and set aside in bowl
  8. Bring skillet to medium-high heat, drain and add chicken
  9. Cook chicken until browned or slightly charred (about 10 min)
  10. Return onion and peppers to skillet
  11. (optional) Add black beans
  12. Mix well and cook to taste

Cook Tortillas

  1. Bring pan to medium-high heat
  2. Rub butter over hot pan and lay tortillas in pan
  3. When tortillas brown on bottom, rub butter on top and flip
  4. Remove from heat when brown to taste on both sides
  5. Repeat until all tortillas cooked

Combine and Serve

  • Finely dice red and green onion
  • Add fajita mixture on top of tortillas
  • Sprinkle red onion, green onion, lime juice, and cotija cheese to taste
  • Enjoy!
