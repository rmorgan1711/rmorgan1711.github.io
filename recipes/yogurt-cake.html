Yogurt Cake (Gâteau au Yaourt)

Ingredients

  • Plain whole-milk yogurt, 2 6-oz containers
  • Sugar, 1 or 2 containers
  • Flour, 4 containers
  • Baking powder, 1½ tsp
  • Eggs, 2
  • Vegetable oil, just under 1 container
  • Vanilla, 1 tsp
  • Optional
    • Frozen berries, 2 containers
      OR
    • Chocolate chips, 2 containers

Credit Where Due

From page 79 of Pamela Druckerman's wonderful book Bringing Up Bébé.
ISBN-10: 0143122967 | ISBN-13: 978-0143122968

Kitchen Gear

  • Bowls, 2
  • 9-inch round cake pan (or loaf pan)

Commentary

  • Use one of the 6-oz yogurt containers to measure sugar, flour, and vegetable oil ingredients
  • A toddler can prepare most of this dish with appropriate supervision
  • Cake should be almost crispy on the outside and springy on the inside

Cook

  1. Preheat oven to 375° F
  2. In bowl, gently combine
    • Yogurt
    • Eggs
    • Sugar
    • Vanilla
    • Vegetable oil
  3. In separate bowl, mix
    • Flour
    • Baking powder
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients
  5. Mix gently until ingredients are just combined (don't overmix)
  6. Mix in optional ingredient if desired
  7. Bake for 35 min (5 min more if cake doesn't pass the knife test)
  8. Let cool
  9. Enjoy!
