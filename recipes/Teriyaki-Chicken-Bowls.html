Teriyaki Chicken Bowls

Cook chicken

  1. In Instapot or pressure cooker, add
    • 1 lb of chicken
    • chicken broth until chicken is nearly covered
    • teriyaki sauce to taste, around 1/2 cup
    • 3 tbsp of taco seasoning
  2. Mix ingredients until distributed evenly
  3. Cook 25 minutes under pressure

Cook Rice

  1. In a rice cooker, add
    • 2 cups rice (I usually use medium grain white rice)
    • 4 cloves minced garlic (or 2 tbsp garlic powder)
    • water to preferred moisture, around 2.25 cups
    • 3 drops of lemon juice
  2. Start the rice cooking

Cook Vegatables

  1. Add 4 tbsp of vegetable oil to a cast iron skillet and bring to medium heat
  2. Once the pan is hot, add
    • 1 cup roughly chopped peppers
    • 1 roughly chopped white onion
  3. Stir occasionally until the onion is wilted then add
    • Add 2 - 3 finely minced chipotle peppers
    • 6 oz frozen cut green beans
  4. Cook until green beans are done to taste and reduce to low head until chicken is done cooking

Combine and Serve

  1. When the chicken is done, roughly chop and mix into the cast iron skillet
  2. Add skillet mixture over a bowl of rice and enjoy
